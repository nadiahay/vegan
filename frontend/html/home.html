Hello and welcome!

Different animals hold different meanings for different people, and our relationship with them is shaped by various factors—culture, religion, upbringing, personality, environment, etc. For some, a steak is nothing more than a delicious meal, only worth considering when deciding how long to cook it. For others, it symbolizes suffering, cruelty, and loss. Fox fur may be seen as a sign of luxury by some, while for others, it serves as a painful reminder of the unnecessary harm inflicted on vulnerable animals. For some, cow's milk might be the perfect addition to a morning coffee,yet for others, it represents the heartache of a mother being separated from her baby.

At least, most people can agree on loving dogs and cats… well, except in some parts of Asia, but we won’t get into that.

Whether you cherish animals, exploit them, or rarely think about them, I invite you to take just a few minutes to reflect—with empathy and an open mind—on their lives while you're here.
