There's so many ways to help, here is what you can do

Adopt a Pet

Adopting a pet is a great way to help animals in need. There are many animals in shelters that need a loving home. You can find a pet to adopt on websites like Petfinder and Adopt-a-Pet.

Try Being Vegan

Going vegan is a great way to help animals. You can try being vegan for a day, a week, or even a month. There are many resources online to help you get started.

You can try a vegan plan! Here are some examples:

1. Veggie´s don’t bite 1 week vegan meal plan
2. PETA’s 2 week vegan meal plan
3. Forks ober knives 1 week vegan meal plan
4. the real food dietitians 2 week vegan meal plan
5. PETA’s 3 week vegan meal plan

Buy Cruelty Free Products

Animal testing is a horrible thing that is still happening today. Don't buy products that are tested on animals. There are many companies that are cruelty-free. You can find a list of cruelty-free companies on websites like PETA and Cruelty-Free Kitty. Watch Save Ralph
